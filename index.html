About Marius

Hello, I'm Marius.

I'm a creative director and photographer based in Germany. I love capturing moments that tell stories -- from sweeping landscapes to quiet street corners.

Double-click the photos on the desktop to explore my work. Feel free to drag them around just like a real desktop.

This portfolio is an homage to the classic Macintosh -- the machine that started it all for creative professionals everywhere.
